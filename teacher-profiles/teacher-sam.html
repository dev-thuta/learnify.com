My Creative Journey

Hello! I'm Firefly, an artist and educator with a passion for color, imagination, and helping others discover their creative spark. I believe everyone has an artist within them, waiting to be awakened. My classes are playful, encouraging, and focused on the joy of creation rather than just the final product. With 8 years of experience teaching diverse groups, from tiny tots to seasoned adults, I aim to make art accessible and fun for everyone.
